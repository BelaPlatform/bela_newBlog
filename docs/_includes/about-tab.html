About this blog

[Bela](http://bela.io) is the platform for ultra-low latency audio and sensor processing. Bela was initially funded on [Kickstarter](http://bit.do/belaKickstarter) in April 2016, and has been growing ever since.

This blog is for longer and more in-depth information about Bela projects and features. For support and to join our worldwide community, please visit the [Bela forum](http://forum.bela.io) where you can join the conversation!

Bela is a project of Augmented Instruments Ltd. Please follow us on [Twitter](http://twitter.com/belaProject) and [Facebook](http://www.facebook.com/belaProject), and see all of our code, tutorials and examples on [Github](http://github.com/belaProject).
